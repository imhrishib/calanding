Terms and Conditions

The Campus Ambassador program is only for college students. Certificate of Commemoration will only be given to campus ambassadors, if he/she fulfills the necessary criteria, deemed fit by the organiser. Alcheringa, IIT Guwahati will be providing all the publicity material to the respective college, but will not be responsible for any extra cost incurred by the campus ambassador. A Campus Ambassador is entitled to 100% travel reimbursement (sleeper class), only if he/she manages to conduct city elimination or bring in a team of size 15 or more. Tri-weekly winners will be announced every 3 weeks, whereas the grand prize of top 10 campus ambassadors will be given during the festival itself. Points will be fetched, depending on your acceptance or rejection of idea, POCs. Changes subjected to Team Alcheringa, however it will be notified through the portal, do check the portal from time to time. A maximum of 2 campus ambassadors from each college. Some extra points will be given to the individuals, if he/she helps in conducting eliminations.
